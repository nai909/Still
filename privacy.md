# Privacy Policy for Still

**Last updated: February 2026**

## Overview

Still is a meditation and mindfulness app designed with your privacy in mind. We believe in complete transparency about how your data is handled.

## Data Collection

**Still does not collect any personal data.**

- No account or login required
- No personal information collected
- No usage analytics or tracking
- No advertising or ad networks
- No third-party data sharing

## Local Storage

Still stores your preferences (such as color theme and last-used settings) locally on your device only. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any server
- Is not accessible to us or any third party
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

## Permissions

Still uses haptic feedback to enhance your meditation experience. This feature:

- Does not access any personal data
- Does not require special permissions
- Can be disabled in your device settings

## Children's Privacy

Still does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
